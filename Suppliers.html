Want to find local suppliers in the cambridgeshire and the surrounding areas?

Here you will find some hand picked suppliers I have used for my wedding that I can highly reccomend! This will help plan you day and give you peace of mind that the suppliers can been reccomended.


Food!

cacciatores food fired pizza...




Have you thought about evening food?

When your evening guests arrive they might not have eaten have you considered food for them.
I can highly reccomend cacciatores food fired pizza! They are extremely professional and put you at ease with their organisation.
Their set up includes an actual clay pizza oven on a horsebox and a tent set up with pizzas laid out with labels.
They cater for vegans and veggie which was a bonus and you can customise you pizza.
We had a custom wedding named pizza where we hand picked the ingredients that went on it!


Venue

Suffolk retreats...




Want a flexible venue?

This is a blank canvas with some help. If you want flexibility but don't want all of the stress of organising everything. This venue is the perfect base

It has toilets on site and you can rent a marquee, chairs, tables and the dance floor from here or organise your own.

There is also accomedation with a house sleeping 10 people and yurts to sleep another 10 people

There is no corkage on drinks and you can even hire a bouncy castle and garden games from the place.


Another Venue

Hedingham Castle...




Want a classy Venue?

If you can afford it Hedingham Castle is a stunning venue. I highly reccomend it. You can also opt to add optional extras, we chose to have owls as our ring bearer! We loved this venue but it couldn't provide the flexibility we wanted such as supply our own drink and food and no garden games were allowed. So we decided to have this as our morning venue where the ceremony took place and in the afternoon moved to suffolk retreats.


Bar

Oliver Platt events...




Want a your own bar?

If you want to provide your own alcohol and all you need is staff and a bar why don't you use Oliver Platt events they are extremely friendly and take the stress out of the day by providing handy staff to help. Optional extras they provide include a firepit and haybales.


Hair & Make-up

Denise Lilley makeup...




Want a classic look on your wedding day?

I used Denise for my hair and make up. She was lovely and extremely professional made me feel at ease and know exactly what would look good on me. I would highly recommned her!


Wedding vehicle

Classic cool campers...




Have you considered how to will get between venues?

If you want a cool and quirky way to getween you ceremony and evening venue what about a campervan. We hired Gino and we didn't regret it!

Day Food

Bizzibeans Catering...

If you want casual day food how about a BBQ. Bizzibeans is great they set up a bbq and serve up the food like a buffet so your guests can help themselves.


Confetti

Daisy Shop...

Need confetti?. Use Daisy Shop to buy your own online and then you can make your own cones from paper dollys or just leave in as self serve from a basket!


Something quirky?

Coda Falconry...

Want something unique on your wedding day consider using Coda Falconry experiences. You can hire a owl for your ring bearer and get falconry shows for your wedding guests! Guests can also pose and meet the owls! Thsi was one of the highlights of my wedding! Definitely reccomend it!
