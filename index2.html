Planning a wedding can be so stressful! Take my word for it! I want to try and take away some of the stress. This website is designed to help give you some ideas of how to plan a DIY wedding. I had my wedding June 2019 and designed it exactly the way I wanted it meaning I had full control of all aspects and didn't need to go with the venue's food or drink, I was able to supply my own drink with no corkage fees and choose all the entertainment

What we do?

This website includes local suppliers and venues based in the cambridgeshire area. The website will also cover a drinks calculator, wedding tweets and instagram to give you some inspirational ideas. There is also a checklist of some things you should consider including in your wedding.
